Hi folks!


This is my very first blog post, and I'm really excited to finally begin this new chapter! I've decided to start this blog because I needed to talk about what I like the most and share my thoughts on the matter. In this space, I'll be sharing posts on cybersecurity and what may revolve around it.


Lately, I've become deeply hooked on CTF challenges. My first encounter with them was last year during a course at university, and I've been fascinated ever since. Expect to see a lot of writeups where I'll share my solutions and problem-solving approaches as well.


Feel free to join me, share your thoughts, or just join in on the discussion. I'd like to connect with others who have similar interests.


Have good readings!
