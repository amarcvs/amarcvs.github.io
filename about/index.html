About me

I'm Marco and I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at "La Sapienza" University of Rome. Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Engineering in Computer Science at the same University, with a focus on Cybersecurity, a field I am deeply passionate about.

During my academic journey, I had the opportunity to work as an IT Consultant for a Digital Transformation company, where I contributed to a large-scale project in Italy. Through this experience, I developed hands-on expertise in consulting and handling complex projects.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy participating in Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges and exploring the world of Open Source software, where I find great fulfillment in both learning and contributing to the community.

Projects

Over the past few years, I have explored various areas of computer science, working with a wide range of technologies. I've developed a particular interest in back-end systems, C programming language and Linux.
