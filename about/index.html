About me

I'm Marco and I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at "La Sapienza" University of Rome. Currently, I'm pursuing a Master's degree in Engineering in Computer Science at the same University, with a focus on Cybersecurity concepts, which has become a passion of mine.

During my studies, I had the opportunity to work for a period as an IT Consultant in a Digital Transformation Company on a large-scale project in Italy.

In general, I strive to create reliable and robust solutions that ensure, first and foremost, security against malicious attacks, by putting myself in the shoes of the bad guys who act behind the scenes. In addition to Cybersecurity, I have a keen interest in the field of microcontrollers.

Outside of study, I like playing CTF challenges and gradually explore the world of Open Source software.

Projects

I have spent the last few years studying in different areas of computer science using different technologies and I was especially attracted to those for the back-end of a computer system. I particularly love the C programming language and Linux.

You can find some of the projects I have created, or contribute to, in the work section of this website and more on my GitHub page.
